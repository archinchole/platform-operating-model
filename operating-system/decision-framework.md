# Decision Framework

## Objective

Define how decisions are made at scale.

---

## Decision Levels

### Strategic (Leadership)

- Platform direction  
- Investment priorities  

---

### Tactical (Engineering Leads)

- Architecture patterns  
- Capability design  

---

### Operational (Teams)

- Implementation details  
- Execution choices  

---

## Key Principle

> Decisions should be made as close to execution as possible,
within clearly defined boundaries.

---

## Anti-Pattern

- Centralized approvals  
- Slow decision cycles  
- Dependency bottlenecks
