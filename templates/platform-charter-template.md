# Platform Charter Template

## Purpose

Define the scope, ownership, and expected outcomes of a platform initiative.

This ensures clarity and alignment before implementation begins.

---

## 1. Platform Vision

- What problem does the platform solve?  
- What outcomes are expected?  
- How does it align with business goals?  

---

## 2. Scope

- What capabilities are included?  
- What is explicitly out of scope?  

---

## 3. Ownership

- Who owns the platform?  
- Who consumes it?  
- What are the responsibilities of each?  

---

## 4. Capabilities

List the core capabilities the platform provides:

- Capability 1  
- Capability 2  
- Capability 3  

---

## 5. Adoption Model

- How will teams adopt the platform?  
- Is adoption optional or guided?  
- What incentives exist for adoption?  

---

## 6. Governance

- What is standardized?  
- What remains flexible?  
- How are decisions made?  

---

## 7. Success Metrics

Define measurable outcomes:

- Reduction in duplication  
- Improvement in delivery predictability  
- Increase in reuse  
- Faster onboarding  

---

## 8. Risks and Dependencies

- What could slow adoption?  
- What dependencies exist across teams?  

---

## 9. Evolution Plan

- How will the platform evolve over time?  
- How will feedback be incorporated?  

---

## Summary

A platform without a clear charter becomes:

- underutilized  
- misunderstood  
- difficult to scale  

Clarity at the start prevents fragmentation later.
